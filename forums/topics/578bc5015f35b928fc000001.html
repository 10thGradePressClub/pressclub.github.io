Apple Supreme Overlord
updated 6 months ago

Hi everyone,

I am sad to inform you that Overcast will be shutting down over the next month and a half. On September 5th the Minecraft servers will be turned off and the website will be put into archive mode. The past four and a half years have been more than Steve and I could have ever asked for when we started Overcast in 2012 after watching a dewtroid livestream together. Overcast has been shrinking for the past two years ever since Steve and I went to college. We have always wanted to keep the network alive and pushed for survival. Seeing the community dwindle over the past two years has been painful. We were unable to hire more developers, advertise the server, and grow the community. It’s time to shutdown.

Overcast wouldn’t have been possible without the help of so many talented and generous individuals. I’d like to thank Cubist for being our dedicated and hardworking lead developer and technical administrator. Thanks to khazhyk, Plastix, H0bie, YukonAppleGeek, CacklingPanda, McSpider, and McYukon for so many laughs and so much support. Thanks to Brett, Torn_Ares, Martin, Brottweiler, Pbat, Parkway, and ElectroidFilms for your incredible leadership and perseverance. Thanks to the dozens of Map Developers for your endless amounts of innovation and creativity. Thanks to the Event Coordinators for putting on so many enjoyable events. Thanks to all our Moderators, Translators, Builders, Officials, Refs, and other community members for all the memories.

For shutdown logistics, please read the Shutdown FAQ post below.

Thanks again for everything,
Apple and Anxuiz

Personal Statements
Below are some statements that our admins wanted to share.

BrettOgden:

Over the last almost four years Overcast has meant a lot to me personally. We’re an international community made up of many talented people. I am proud of what we accomplished together, thank you for that. This experience has been life changing for me, I’ve made many friends, learned a lot, grew as a person, & became more social. This journey will forever be with me and I’m sad to see it end.

Hopefully I was able to live up to your expectations while serving our community, as my heart was always touched when I could bring joy to others. The progression from player to admin meant a lot to me. Even with all the mistakes, and yes there were a lot I wouldn’t trade the experience for anything. As for my future plans, I’m not sure at this point. I’ve always had an entrepreneur spirit, so it’s likely that I’ll launch an internet company soon.

Now for some thank you’s

  • MonsieurApple - Thank you for starting Project Ares back in 2012. Without you none of us would have experienced this amazing journey. I’ve personally witnessed you sacrifice so much for the enjoyment of others that words can’t begin to describe it. You have always been someone I’ve admired due to your kind heart & giving attitude
  • Anxuiz - Even though we’ve never worked closely together, you’re a kool kat. Thanks for everything you did for Project Ares
  • Khazyhk - Thank you for entrusting me with the events team. You built the foundation & we built upon it ever since. Due to your hard work & dedication we were able to host 16 events for thousands of players. The skills I’ve learned while working events will always be useful, without you I wouldn’t have those.
  • ElectroidFilms - You have always been there for me, you’ve been an amazing friend, and I’m proud to have worked alongside you for so many years. Your future endeavors will be amazing to witness. Once you’re done with college, expect me to come knocking with some crazy idea.
  • Majjus_ - You stepped up into a pretty hard job, you’re a rockstar & I wish you well in life. I know you’re very talented & I couldn’t have done it without you by myside. Thank you.

My Events Staff - Even though at times you drove (or continue to drive) me crazy, I cherish the time I got to spend with each & every one of you. The memories are everlasting, especially when Aceman1998 forgot something during referee training. You guys are one special bunch of people that I consider friends.

The community as a whole - Thank you for playing & enjoying OCN. Without you I wouldn’t have been able to have these amazing experiences & make so many new friends.

This may be the end for OCN but remember this doesn’t have to be the end of your friendships with others OCN players. You can still enjoy other networks & games with them. This is a sad process to go though, but let’s make the best of it. Please choose to be happy & cherish the memories instead of ending this amazing ride on a salty note.

Sincerely with respect,
Brett

Martin:

Being part of this network has been such a great experience. I'm incredibly sad that it has to end.

Apple, Anxuiz, and all of the original admin team - thank you for what you created and built here. You've given hours of entertainment to hundreds of thousands of people all over the world, but most of all you created a community and changed lives for the better along the way. You should be very proud of what OCN achieved.

The current devs, Cubist and Electroid (as well as all of the past devs) - thank you for your incredible work on the network. Without you Overcast would have floundered long ago. The improvements we've seen in PGM over the past couple of years have been enormous.

And the rest of my fellow admins, Torn, Brott, Pbat, Parkway and Brett - it's been a massive pleasure to work alongside you, even though it's coming to an end too soon. I consider all of you good friends and I think we worked great as a team.

To all the other staff, current and former - thanks for being such awesome people. I know how much work many of you put into Overcast over a long time, and even though the network is ending it's important to remember that it was all worth it for the fun and enjoyment we were able to give to so many people.

Special mention is due to Brott, Parkway and Cratain for always being so entertaining to talk to on Slack, even during stressful times. I definitely owe it to you guys for keeping me sane.

To the community - thanks for always being so passionate about the network. Thanks for all of the suggestions, criticisms and positive feedback that you gave us. Most of all, thank you for being a part of this place.

There have been times when it's been incredibly hard to be a staff member here. There's been plenty of tedious, mundane work. There have been tough decisions and difficult interactions and there have been times when it's become so overwhelming I've considered quitting. But at the end of the day, I'm glad I was able to play a part, however small, in something so unique and special.

Now, we have only a few weeks left to enjoy this network, so let's make the most of them. I'm taking a vacation for a bit, but when I get back I'll definitely be playing as much as I can. See you all on the battlefield.

Parkway:

So long, and thanks for all the fish.

I’ve been sitting here trying to think of something heartfelt, unique, and interesting as a farewell, but there’s nothing I can say that others haven’t already said or will be said in the upcoming replies. Your goodbyes as the community are just as important as ours. Sure, we had the fancy yellow (people tell me they’re orange) names, and sure, we had the final say in things, and SURE, we had to put up with all of you, but in the end, a network doesn’t run on the backs of 8 people. Without the rest of you, this simply doesn’t exist, and there would be nothing to say goodbye to. Thank you all for being here and being what Overcast is.

Thanks to Martin especially for being an unexpectedly close friend as we worked our way through this whole journey.

Apple Supreme Overlord

Shutdown FAQ
We will update this post as more questions arise.

  • Why is Overcast shutting down?
    • We decided it’s time to move on. The playerbase has been shrinking for over two years and we can no longer afford to keep Overcast online. A lack of players and money means we can no longer afford to develop Overcast or keep the servers online.
  • Why didn’t you sell Overcast to another network?
    • We will entertain any serious business inquiries that are sent to [email protected] during the process of shutting down.
      • Don't offer to run Overcast as your personal hobby, it's too expensive.
  • Why not accept donations to keep the server alive?
    • We were ran off of donations in the beginning, it wasn’t sustainable. We considered this when the new administration took over but it wouldn’t be enough.
  • Will any purchases be refunded?
    • All purchases made on or after June 1st will be refunded. It is unlikely we will refund purchases made before June 1st but feel free to email [email protected].
  • When will the Minecraft servers turn off?
    • The Minecraft servers will no longer accept connections on September 5th.
  • When will the website turn off?
    • On September 5th the website will be put into archive mode. No new forum posts, appeals, reports, or other changes will be allowed. The website will remain online for a while after September 5th. We have not decided when we will turn the website off for good. If and when an exact date is determined we will make an announcement.
  • Will other services turn off? (e.g. Mumble)
    • Mumble will remain online as long as the website is online. Therefore, Mumble will likely remain online for a while after September 5th. If and when an exact date is determined we will make an announcement.
  • Will the code become open sourced?
    • We have not decided what to do with the source code. Once a decision is reached we will make an announcement.
  • What is happening with the next tournament?
    • More info here
  • Is there an event planned for the last day of Minecraft servers?
    • We are most likely going to plan a Nostalgia event sometime before shutting down the Minecraft servers. We’ll update you when these plans are made.

I have too many people to thank. If you have played on a team with me, I am thankful to you, for laughing alongside me. If you have ever spoken with me, I am thankful to you, for keeping me company. If you have ever just been in a match with me, working on the same bridge, digging out the same pit, fighting off the same offender, I am thankful to you. So many of you have touched my life in ways I would never have thought unimaginable. The friendships I have made were always my driving force in coming back to Overcast, and I will treasure them always.

I have too many people to apologize to. I am well aware of my inadequacies. I am stubborn, proud, and at times blind to what must be done. For every time I have caused one of you stress or sadness, I am truly sorry. I have wanted nothing more than to, in whatever way I could, be of slight help to this server. I have wanted nothing more than to contribute to another player’s joy, as the work of previous players contributed to mine.

Finally, I have too many people left to meet. In the final days of this server, I have a request, for all of you: choose happiness. I realize that this news may seem abrupt to some of you, I realize that others will be patting themselves on the back saying “I told you so.” You are free to take your time processing, in whatever way you wish. But once you are done, come play.

You who have spoken of decline, you who have longed for what is long gone: in the final moments of this server, bring back what you have wanted. I remember matches stacked on both sides with the best players around, edging out one another after 2 hours in a oportune moment of luck and skill. I remember hoping on to Mumble to meet new people, reluctantly at first, but with increasing confidence. This server will close soon, but not immediately. With what time is left, hop on and make a defence, bigger and better than ever before. Go into Mumble and talk to someone you have never spoken to. Bring your friends back into a call and see what crazy things you can do. Life is in a constant state of flux, with things beginning and ending–all we can do is laugh ourselves onwards, looking back fondly on what was, and gazing forward at what is yet to become. Enjoy what time you have left–I will be doing my best to do just the same.
