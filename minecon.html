Hey everyone,

UPDATE:

For those who missed the panel, you may view it on the Minecon YouTube livestream. Amazing job Overcast, Hypixel, and Shotbow!

GENERAL NOTES:

  • COME TO THE PANEL. It's SUNDAY at 11:40 AM on the third floor! Look at your schedules for "Building Server Communities." This is where we will be giving away the majority of the FREE T-SHIRTS!
  • Follow me (@Monsieur_Apple) on twitter for the latest info tidbits. I will try to update this thread as well.
  • You can also follow @YukonAppleGeek or @khazhyk for other MINECON news.

UPDATE ON OCTOBER 31ST:

Since https://oc.tc/minecon leads here, I will be updating this post with important MINECON related information.

UPDATE ON OCTOBER 31ST:

THE SHIRTS ARE IN: https://twitter.com/Monsieur_Apple/status/395656384194150400

My flight leaves tomorrow at 7AM. Fun stuff. I've got the 200 shirts to giveaway in tow. If you're not going to MINECON, we're going to add them to the website shop a few weeks after MINECON.

Also, if you're going to MINECON, have a friend that's going to MINECON, or know someone at MINECON, tell them to go to the "Building Server Communities" panel at 10:40 AM on Sunday! It's featuring me, khazhy, and Yukon from Overcast, AgentK and codename_B from Hypixel, and LazerTester and HighlifeTTU from shotbow! We want a huge turn out and that's where we will be giving away most of the free T-shirts. PLEASE COME TO THE PANEL! THIRD FLOOR! Hope to see you all there!

Also, if you see me, please don't be shy! I'd love to say hi and talk :D

OLD POST:

A few things about MINECON 2013. First off, Yukon, Khaz, and I will be there and we will be presenting a panel with Hypixel and Shotbow! Announcement tweet: https://twitter.com/OvercastNetwork/status/392460348009115648

Anyways, I'd like to get some shirts made to give away while we're there. So, does anyone have any artwork or shirt ideas? Please post here or email [email protected]! I'm going to send the shirt stuff off to the printer Wednesday. I'm guessing we'll print like 200 or so shirts and give them away to Overcastians at MINECON!

Yay :D
