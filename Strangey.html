About Strangey














I pretty much found the server off a livestream of Sand Wars and I wanted to play it and 
boom, loved OCN forever. I eventually became #2 in kills and #1 in deaths and playing time 
which kinda made me well known in the community. Once tourneys were announced, I formed the 
team Impact and got as far as being DQ'd in a finals match due to not knowing a team member 
was toggle sprinting. A year later, I worked on maps for the server as something to do in 
my free time and I eventually won my 1st and 2nd tourney with my team. Then out of the 
blue, I somehow get modded and got mapdev in the same weekend in December. I've gotten into 
making Youtube videos and I have a pretty good audience of 1,400+ subscribers!
